The Space

Inside Dora's

Two dining rooms, a full cantina bar, and murals you'll want to take photos of. Come for the food — stay for the vibe.

The Mural Room

Hand-painted walls celebrate the art, music, and soul of Mexico — sugar skulls, tequila, maracas, and a portrait of Selena herself watching over every table.

The Cantina Bar

Neon-lit, fully stocked, and decked in papel picado. Ice-cold beer on tap, every game on screen.
